What Is a Licensed Home Care Services Agency?

A Licensed Home Care Services Agency, or LHCSA, provides professional in-home care services to individuals who pay privately or have private insurance coverage. This model allows patients to receive personal support and skilled care while remaining in a familiar home environment.

For families, that means access to aides, nurses, therapy professionals, and home support services through one coordinated agency.
