About the Agency

Helping patients remain safe, comfortable, and independent at home.

1st Aide Home Care, Inc. is a Licensed Home Care Services Agency focused on high-quality in-home care for individuals who pay privately or use private insurance coverage. We support short-term recovery, long-term assistance, and day-to-day living needs through a coordinated team.

Families often come to us when they need more than a list of services. They need a responsive partner who can listen, recommend a care approach, and put dependable support in place.

Trusted care starts with clear communication.

We coordinate services thoughtfully, answer questions directly, and help families move from uncertainty to a workable care plan.
